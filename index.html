E-COMMERCE

The E-commerce website, is, as could be inferred, an app mimicking the appearance,functionality and purpouse of a typical e-commerce website.The functionalities range from browsing a wide array of products, selecting ones the customer desires to purchase, and proceeding with the checkout.

TEXT'EM

Chat with friends and strangers alike, is the motto of the Text'em chat app. It offers a variety of features but the main and the most important one is the ability to take part in realtime chats with other logged in users. Due to the use of websockets the connection is seamless and navigation intuitive!

MUSIC APP

Music-App is a Spotify Clone music app for streaming music and manipulating music data. It has all of the features typical for spotify, such as playing songs, creating playlists and listening to favourite artists or their albums. The free version doesn't include streaming music so make sure you have a premium Spotify account.

SOCIALLY AWKWARD

Socially Awkward is a social media type app, meant for socializing with other users by posting images or interacting with others' posts in various ways such as liking them or commenting. The frontend side of the project is built with Angular, and the backend has been created with vanilla PHP.
